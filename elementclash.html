Privacy Policy

ElementClash App | Last updated: March 22, 2026

ElementClash is a fully offline game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All game data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

ElementClash does not collect any personal data. The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Game preferences (selected board theme, sound settings)
  • No accounts, usernames, or personal information is required

2. Gameplay Data

All game data including settings, theme preferences, and game state is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. ElementClash does not connect to any external game servers or databases. The game functions entirely offline.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. No data is transmitted over the internet. No cloud storage or synchronization is used.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. Since all data is stored locally, only you have access to it.

5. Third-Party Services

ElementClash does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline.

6. Children's Privacy

ElementClash is a strategy game suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal data or communicate with external servers, the app is safe for users under 13 years of age. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app data from Android Settings → Apps → ElementClash → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app (this permanently removes all data)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
