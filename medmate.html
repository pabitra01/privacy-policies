Privacy Policy

MedMate App | Last updated: March 20, 2026

MedMate is a fully offline app. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

MedMate does not collect any personal data. The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • User profile name
  • Medication details (names, dosages, forms, schedules)
  • Dose tracking history (taken, skipped, missed records)
  • App preferences and settings (theme preference)

2. Health Data

All medication and health-related information (medication names, dosages, schedules, and adherence records) is stored exclusively on your device in a local SQLite database. MedMate does not connect to any healthcare provider, pharmacy, or medical service. The app is a personal record-keeping tool only and does not provide medical advice.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. No data is transmitted over the internet. No cloud storage or synchronization is used.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. Since all data is stored locally, only you have access to it.

5. Third-Party Services

MedMate does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline.

6. Children's Privacy

MedMate is designed for users of all ages who want to track their medications. Since we do not collect any personal data or communicate with external servers, the app is safe for users under 13 years of age. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app data from Android Settings → Apps → MedMate → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app (this permanently removes all data)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
